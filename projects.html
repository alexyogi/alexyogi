    Sewing Machine Project

    February 2024 - May 2024

  • I worked with a team of five other students to design over 40 individual parts and subassemblies to create a working sewing machine in SolidWorks
  • I was in charge of the threading system and outer casing, which encases and protects the internal components
  • During this project, we also utilized finite element analysis to test the strength and durability of our machine, specifically the foot pedal, and make improvements to our design

    Over Terrain Vehicle Project

    January 2023 - May 2023

  • I collaborated with a group of eight students to design, build, and test an autonomous over terrain vehicle on a budget of $320 over three months as a part of the Intro to Engineering Design course
  • The OTV has to complete an obstacle course and a unique mission that is assigned to each group, which was extracting data from a pylon
  • I designed and built the vehicle chassis, keeping it within the weight and mission constraints
  • Used Fusion360 to render the chassis, as well as the rest of the vehicle
  • Used Prusa Slicer and 3D printers to design and print many of the necessary components, including the data extraction arm, gears, and motor mounts
